Airport rides — please read: SU-Go is a free introduction service that connects students with fellow students who are willing to drive to the airport. SU-Go is not a transport operator, does not employ or formally vet drivers, does not own vehicles, and does not set or collect ride fares. Any ride, fare and arrangement is made directly between you and the driver, at your own discretion and risk. This is a voluntary student-to-student arrangement — a choice given by a student, to a student, for a student — and is not a commercial taxi or e-hailing service. Please use your own judgement, confirm your driver's details, check that they are properly licensed and insured, and share your trip plans with someone you trust.
